Headphones

Circumaural headphones are designed to fit around your ears, rather than sitting on them. This helps to reduce the pressure on your ears and can prevent pain felt by wearing standard over-the-ear types. Circumaural is just a fancy word for "around the ear."

Microphone

A microphone is a device used to capture audio by converting sound waves into electrical signals. These signals may be amplified as an analog signal or digitally converted into a signal that can then be processed by a computer or other digital audio device.
